The Problem

Elderly individuals are often at risk of unnoticed health issues or accidents. Whether it's a sudden fall or a significant health event, the lack of constant monitoring leaves too much to chance. Families are left in a constant state of worry, unsure if their loved ones are safe. There is a clear need for a system that not only detects emergencies but also ensures that help can arrive in time.
